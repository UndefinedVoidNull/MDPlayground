c.
- `simplify(expr)`
- `diff(expr, x) `
- `diff(expr, x, 2)` # second derivative
- `integrate(expr, x)` # indefinite integral
- `integrate(expr, (x, 0, 2))` # definite integral
- `equation = Eq(x**2 - 5*x + 6, 0)`  # define a equation
- `solutions = solve(equation, x)`
